Hello, My name is Narges Gazazani. I'm a Graphic Designer and UI&UX Designer based in Iran With over four years of experience, I excel at translating clients’ visions into creative, impactful designs that drive results.

My proficiency in industry-standard software, UX/UI design, logo creation, branding, and photography allows me to deliver polished solutions tailored to each project’s unique goals. Known for my keen eye for detail and innovative ideas, I consistently produce high-quality work that captivates and satisfies clients.

Let’s collaborate to bring your brand to life with designs that stand out and resonate.
